### Prof. Marie van Staveren

Community Director

*Associate Teaching Professor*  
**University of Maryland, Baltimore County**

Marie van Staveren teaches physical and introductory chemistry labs at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. They received their bachelor's degree in 2007 from the University of Michigan and their PhD in 2012 from UC Irvine. Prof. van Staveren chose to move straight into teaching, working at institutions of various types – public and private, 2- and 4- year – where they gained experience teaching chemistry in many contexts. Since joining the faculty at UMBC in 2017, they have worked to create a UMBC chemistry curriculum where all students feel excited to use code in their own scientific lives, resulting in the development of a framework for programing pedagogy and belonging. Additionally, Prof. van Staveren has experience building community beyond the classroom through both formal and informal learning communities at UMBC and across the country.
